Frequently Asked Questions

What are the Qualifications of Teachers?

All our teachers are qualified and trained in Early Childhood Education. Our head teachers hold a Master’s degree, and all educators are certified and experienced in handling young learners with care and professionalism.

How can I get involved in my child’s classroom?

Parents are encouraged to participate in school activities. Once your child settles in, there will be opportunities to engage through events, celebrations, and school programs conducted throughout the year.

What are the school fees?

Our fee structure is affordable and varies depending on the program. Please contact the school office for detailed information.

Do you offer half-day programs?

Yes, we offer flexible program options including half-day and full-day schedules to suit parents’ needs.

What is the age group for admission?

We accept children from 2.5 to 6 years, covering Playgroup, Pre-KG, LKG, and UKG.

What are the school timings?

School timings vary by program. Please contact us for the exact schedule based on your child’s class.

What documents are required for admission?

You will need:
Birth Certificate
Aadhar card
Passport-size photographs
Parent ID proof
Address proof

Do you provide food?

We encourage healthy eating habits. Parents may send nutritious food from home.

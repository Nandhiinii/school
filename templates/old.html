About Legend Montessori Play School

Legend Montessori Play School is dedicated to early childhood education by combining the Montessori method with the Play Way approach.

We believe children learn best when they explore freely, learn through play, engage in hands-on activities, and express themselves confidently. Our goal is to build a strong foundation for lifelong learning in a happy and caring environment.

What makes us special

Vision & Mission

  • Our Mission

    To provide a joyful, safe, and nurturing environment where children learn, grow, and develop essential life skills.

  • Our Vision

    To build a strong educational foundation that inspires lifelong learning and confident individuals.

  • Our Philosophy

    We believe in a balanced approach combining Montessori and Play Way methods for complete child development.

Our Learning Approach

Feel The Difference

We focus on making learning joyful, interactive, and meaningful.

  1. Explore Freely

    We encourage children to discover the world independently and build confidence.

  2. Learn Through Play

    Fun activities help children understand concepts naturally while enjoying learning.

  3. Hands-on Activities

    Children engage in practical learning that improves real-life understanding.

  4. Express Confidently

    We create a safe space for children to express creativity and emotions freely.
